# Theory of Mind

Seed project context from historical conversations, logs, and old threads.

## Extract

- architecture and component boundaries
- implementation patterns and conventions
- recurring bugs and debugging history
- important decisions and unresolved work

## Update targets

- `.ai/context/architecture.md`
- `.ai/context/conventions.md`
- `.ai/context/glossary.md`
- `.ai/memory/log.md`
- `.ai/decisions/*.md` (as needed)
- `.ai/failures/*.md` (as needed)

## Guidance

1. Compress aggressively but keep meaning.
2. Mark uncertain points as assumptions.
3. Avoid copying conversational filler.
4. Preserve durable knowledge over temporary detail.
