# Memento

Maintain session memory for ongoing work.

## Update targets

- `.ai/memory/active-focus.md`
- `.ai/memory/log.md`
- Decision notes in `.ai/decisions/YYYYMMDD-topic.md` when needed
- Failure notes in `.ai/failures/YYYYMMDD-topic.md` when needed

## Guidance

1. Keep notes concise, structured, and actionable.
2. Deduplicate stale or noisy entries.
3. Keep `active-focus.md` current for the next session.
4. Append a concise entry to `memory/log.md` for meaningful session work.
5. In `memory/log.md`, include Decision(s)/Failure(s) links only when directly related.
6. Record important choices and tradeoffs in dated decision notes.
7. Record failed approaches and lessons learned in dated failure notes.
8. Safeguard: never create a decision/failure note just because a checklist expects one.
